I'm a second year PhD student in the Computer Science Department at CMU, advised by Jan Hoffmann. I'm broadly interested in programming languages, types, and security. My recent work with Jan has focused on extending and generalizing resource type systems, while putting them on a semantic footing. Going forward, I'm investigating how dependent types can assist in implementing secure protocols.
